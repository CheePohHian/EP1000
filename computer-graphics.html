Rastors & Vectors

What Are Rastors & Vector

Rasters deal with pixels on the screens and are used for photographs and images, while vectors uses mathematical functions and are characterised by clean lines.
In this module, we are tasked to do a simple raster and vector project to familiarise ourselves with the tools we are going to use later in the module.

Photo

Here is a random photo of me. The following process is done using Adobe Photoshop.

Filtering

Due to bad compression of the photo, I have to find ways to reduce the noise (visual distortion) from the photo. I use noise filter to make the photo look smoother and less pixelated. Additional Image

Selective Crop

I use Magic Wand tool to selectively outline my head for cropping. Minor adjustments to the crop are made on the edges of my hair and neck. Additional Image

New Background

Once that is done, I downloaded a random image of a white room and place it behind my cropped image.

Final Look

For a more detailed tutorial on how to photoshop images, I reference this Youtuber to make this profile photo. Learn more

Outlining

This is a image of a Raven. This following process is done using Adobe Illustrator. I use Curvature tool to trace the image. I divided the work into 4 different layers: Head, Body, Wings & Legs.

Fill

After spending 2hrs, the I filled in the color grey

Final Look
