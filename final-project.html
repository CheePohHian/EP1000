Function

What does it do?

It is a Audio Visualiser that uses LED 8x8 Matrix that shines through acrylic sticks and simply changes its color by using frequency information from the audio and feeding this data through the LEDs. Audio waveform visualizers create animated waveforms (audiograms) that shift with the rhythm, loudness, and frequency of the audio. It able to illuminate your room as there will be other features such as operating as a normal glow lamp.
Features

2 Operating Modes

The 2 operating modes are Audio Visualiser and Glow Lamp. How this works is that the Audio Visualiser uses a microphone to receive sound input. Hence LEDs will change its brightness or colours accordingly. The other mode simply works by removing the input from the microphone and operates as normal LEDs.

Lightning Features

There will be various lighting profiles I plan to showcase in this project. As of now there will be 2 lighting profiles. First, there will be "RGB" Profile that cycles the colours continuously. Second is the "Single-colour" Profile that will have LEDs operate at colours set by the user.
